Ingredients

  • 2-3 ripe bananas
  • 1/3 cup melted butter
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • Pinch of salt
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 1 large egg, beaten
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease a 4x8 inch loaf pan.
  2. In a mixing bowl, mash the ripe bananas with a fork until smooth.
  3. Stir the melted butter into the mashed bananas.
  4. Mix in the baking soda and pinch of salt.
  5. Stir in the sugar, beaten egg, and vanilla extract.
  6. Add the flour and mix until just incorporated.
  7. Pour the batter into the greased loaf pan.
  8. Bake in the preheated oven for 60-65 minutes or until a toothpick comes out clean when inserted into the center.
  9. Let the banana bread cool in the pan for a few minutes, then transfer it to a wire rack to cool completely.
